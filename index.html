Empowering Karnataka Through Design and Innovation

The D-CoE at IISc Bangalore directly addresses the 80% failure rate of startups in India by providing structured training in design thinking, prototyping, and Industry 4.0 technologies. For instance, startups mentored at IISc have achieved a higher success rate due to tailored mentorship and hands-on skill-building initiatives. By leveraging the expertise of IISc's DM department, the D-CoE empowers stakeholders across industries to achieve measurable outcomes.

Spark Your Success: Our Innovation Engine

The D-CoE features 10 advanced labs tailored to meet the needs of startups, SMEs, and students. From CNC machining for mechanical prototyping to AR/VR hubs for immersive design, our facilities mirror industry environments, ensuring participants are industry-ready. For instance, the electronic prototyping lab supports IoT device development and PCB fabrication, while the Industry 4.0 lab focuses on digital twins and autonomous systems.
