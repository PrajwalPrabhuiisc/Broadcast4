Spark Your Success: Our Innovation Engine

The D-CoE features 10 advanced facilities tailored to meet the needs of startups, MSMEs, and students. These include a content creation facility, virtual training spaces, and dedicated labs for design thinking, aesthetics and semantics, ergonomics and human factors, mechanical prototyping, design prototyping, electronic prototyping, and business development. For instance, the electronic prototyping facility supports IoT devices, infrastructure to develop robots, and the use of FPGA boards, while the AR/VR hub enables immersive design and simulation. Additionally, the Industry 4.0 lab includes infrastructure for developing and testing collaborative robots (cobots) and autonomous systems. These facilities ensure holistic training, aid experiential learning, and serve as tools to transform ideas into reality.

Training Programs and Certifications

The Centre of Excellence (D-CoE) at IISc offers a robust array of training programs designed to enhance skills in design and Industry 4.0 technologies. By integrating practical hands-on training and theoretical insights, these modules aim to prepare participants for the rapidly evolving demands of the tech and manufacturing sectors.

These in-person programs combine technical and design competencies, providing valuable insights into industry practices.
